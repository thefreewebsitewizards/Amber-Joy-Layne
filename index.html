Comedian & Host

"The Dancing Comedian"

📝 Bio Note

"Amber Joy Layne, aka "The Dancing Comedian," is a versatile entertainer based in South Florida. She captivates audiences with her vibrant distinctive style of physical humor. Layne's two decades of training enables her to seamlessly incorporate her dance skills into her punchlines. Her most notable achievement to date is touring as the opening act for Ilana Glazer of "Broad City", and being on her writing team. Given her bubbly energy as a performer, Amber is often hired to host a variety of events including Bar/Bat Mitzvahs, Weddings, Birthdays, Corporate, Community and Cultural Events, as well as Cabarets, Immersive Experiences and even a Circus."
